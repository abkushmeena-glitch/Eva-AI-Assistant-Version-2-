* Financial difficulties
* Unaware of the rules
* First-time offender
* Special circumstances (e.g., family emergency, health issues)
* Alternative punishment or penalty

* Explain the circumstances that led to your mistake
* Show that you have learned from your mistake and will not repeat it
* Be respectful and polite in your application
* Provide any supporting documents or evidence, if necessary
